## Abstract

PSeq is designed to resolve full-length individual RNA molecules on short-read NGS platforms. The publication introduces the approach to transcript isoform resolution, its automated data-processing workflow, and the molecule-level information available for downstream analysis and delivery.

## Key topics

- Full-length RNA sequencing on short-read NGS
- Quantitative, molecule-resolved transcript isoforms
- Automated data processing and scalable delivery
