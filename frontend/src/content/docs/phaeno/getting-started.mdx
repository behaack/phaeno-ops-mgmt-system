# Phaeno operations guide

The Phaeno organization workspace is the operating surface for organization administration, data provisioning, Customer and Partner order work, commercial configuration, and integration recovery.

## Select the Phaeno organization

Use the organization switcher and select Phaeno before opening platform operations. Customer and Partner contexts intentionally show tenant-facing information and actions instead.

Platform capabilities come from an active administrator membership in the active Phaeno organization. Authentication-provider roles are not used for portal authorization.

## Find platform work

- **Organizations** and **User management** currently preview organization and access administration with session-only mock data. Do not treat changes there as durable; the connected administration client is not yet complete.
- **Data provisioning** manages source samples, curated packages, grants, and governance.
- **Order operations** contains Customer lab, Partner reagent, and Partner assembly queues.
- **Order configuration** contains commercial, scientific, Partner-offering, credit, and integration settings.
- **Documentation** contains Phaeno guides and lets support staff view Customer and Partner instructions.

## Separate safe and internal information

Tenant timelines and messages must contain only Customer- or Partner-safe reasons and actions. Internal notes, scientific investigation evidence, credentials, retry details, and other restricted facts stay in authorized Phaeno surfaces.

Never place passwords, OAuth credentials, webhook secrets, connection strings, protected health information, or customer file contents in notes, audit descriptions, notification messages, or QuickBooks memo fields.

## Support users safely

Confirm the selected organization, user membership, capability, record reference, and visible status before changing data. Use the Customer or Partner documentation view to understand what the caller can see. Do not ask users to share credentials or authentication tokens.
