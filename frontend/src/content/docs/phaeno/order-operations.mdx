# Order operations

Order operations unifies Customer laboratory work, Partner reagent fulfillment, and Partner data assembly without relying on an external ERP or LIMS. Phaeno Portal is the operational source of truth; QuickBooks Online owns commercial documents and payment facts.

## Work from queues

Use workflow type, status, hold, overdue, organization, assignment, and due-date filters to find work. Assign an owner and due date where the workflow supports it. Keep operational status, quote status, commercial synchronization, payment, and file release separate.

Tenant users receive a curated timeline. Record a tenant-safe reason separately from internal notes whenever an action can be seen outside Phaeno.

## Authorize Customer laboratory jobs

Review submitted samples and metadata, request corrections when required, and issue a per-job quote. Customer acceptance records the Commercial authorization and linked Lab work order in one operation.

Use **Lab operations** for receipt, accession, protocol execution, materials, equipment, libraries, batches, outsourced sequencing, exceptions, and scientific approval. **Ready for release** returns a controlled milestone to Commercial but does not publish files. Release only scanned, checksummed, scientifically ready result artifacts. Credit-approved Customers can receive ready sample results under Net 30 after required synchronization. Without approved credit, no job result is downloadable until the completed invoice is paid in QuickBooks.

## Fulfill Partner reagent orders

Review the placed commercial snapshot before acceptance. Record holds or rejection with a Partner-safe reason. A substitution requires Partner-administrator approval, and a total increase requires commercial approval before acceptance.

Create partial or complete shipments with quantities, backorders, carrier, service, tracking, lot, expiration, and packing-slip facts. Shipped quantities remain immutable. Invoice-on-shipment documents are handled through QuickBooks Online.

## Process Partner data assembly

Review the immutable input revision against the selected profile. Request targeted corrections or reject unsafe or unsupported work. Issue a per-job quote, queue accepted work, and record processing and quality review.

Approve an immutable output manifest and files linked to the source revision and profile. Start completion-invoice synchronization, then apply the Partner assembly-credit or paid-status release gate before downloads.

## Handle cancellation and recovery

Preserve work already performed, shipped quantities, prior revisions, releases, and commercial history. Decide cancellation requests as approved, partially approved, or declined and synchronize any adjustment through QuickBooks.

Failed QuickBooks or notification deliveries remain visible and retryable. Retry the durable integration event; do not recreate the order or manually duplicate a commercial document.
