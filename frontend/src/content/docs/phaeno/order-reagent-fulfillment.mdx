# Partner reagent fulfillment

Partner reagent orders use the offering and negotiated price available to the active Partner at placement. The placed order preserves an immutable Commercial snapshot even if catalog or pricing configuration changes later.

## Review a placed order

Confirm the Partner, purchase-order number, shipping address, ordered items, selling units, quantities, negotiated prices, currency, and placement totals. Verify that the shipping destination and requested service satisfy the configured restrictions.

Move valid work through review and acceptance. Place the order on hold or reject it with a Partner-safe reason when the address, eligibility, quantity, or Commercial facts require correction. Keep internal investigation details in the internal record.

## Handle substitutions and backorders

Never substitute a different reagent silently. Record the original item, proposed item, quantity, price effect, and reason. A Partner administrator must explicitly approve the substitution. If the change increases the total, complete the required Commercial approval and QuickBooks adjustment before acceptance.

Backorders remain part of the original order. Record the unshipped quantity and expected next action without changing quantities already shipped.

## Create shipments

Create a partial or complete shipment with:

- shipped quantities for each original order line;
- carrier, service, tracking number, and ship date;
- reagent lot or batch identifier;
- expiration date when the supplied reagent has one; and
- the packing-slip facts.

Shipped quantities are immutable. Correct future fulfillment with a new shipment or documented adjustment; do not edit shipment history.

## Complete fulfillment

Reagent fulfillment is invoice-on-shipment through QuickBooks Online. Partial shipments may create separate invoices tied to the same portal order. QuickBooks remains authoritative for invoice totals and terms. The order reaches fulfillment only when the accepted quantities are shipped, backordered quantities are resolved, and no blocking hold remains.
