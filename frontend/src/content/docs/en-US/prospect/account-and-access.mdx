# Account and access

Your Portal access is created through an invitation to a specific Prospect organization. The current organization, your active membership, and your role determine which data and administrative information is available.

## Accept an invitation

1. Open the newest invitation link sent for your Prospect organization.
2. Sign in with the same email address that received the invitation.
3. Review the organization, your name, and the offered role.
4. Accept or decline the invitation.

Invitation links are private. A link can expire, be revoked, or be replaced by a newer invitation. If it is unavailable, ask your organization administrator or Phaeno to send a new invitation.

The portal does not collect or reset your sign-in password. Use the account recovery offered by the identity service on the sign-in page.

## Confirm the current organization

The user menu shows the organization active in your current session. Every package, download, and administrative view is scoped to it.

Self-service switching between multiple external organization memberships is not currently available. If the wrong organization is shown, stop and contact Phaeno before opening or downloading data.

## Understand roles

- **Member** can review and download package versions explicitly granted to the current Prospect organization.
- **Administrator** can also review organization download history and respond to applicable package-governance instructions.

The authorization model allows Prospect administrators to manage membership only for their organization, but the current **User management** screen is a session-only preview. Changes made there are not sent to the durable invitation and membership APIs. Contact Phaeno for durable invitation, role, deactivation, or reactivation changes.

## Resolve common access states

| What you see | What to do |
| --- | --- |
| Invitation link unavailable | Ask an administrator to confirm that the invitation is still pending and resend it. Use only the newest link. |
| Email does not match | Sign out and use the email address that received the invitation, or request a corrected invitation. |
| No active memberships | Ask your Prospect administrator to coordinate a fresh invitation with Phaeno. |
| Wrong organization shown | Stop before opening data and contact Phaeno to verify the active membership. |
| Organization unavailable | Ask Phaeno whether the organization is inactive. |
| Account disabled | Contact Phaeno. A Prospect administrator cannot override a globally disabled account. |

Never send a password, sign-in code, invitation token, or browser storage value in a support message.
