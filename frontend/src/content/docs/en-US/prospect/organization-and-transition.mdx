# Organization access and transition

A Prospect is a tenant-isolated external organization that may receive explicitly granted curated data but cannot place commercial orders. Its users, memberships, grants, downloads, and audit history remain scoped to that organization.

## Manage organization access

Organization administrators are responsible for identifying who should have access, but the current **User management** screen is a session-only preview. Changes made there do not persist.

Contact Phaeno for durable invitation or membership changes. Provide the organization and person’s exact email address, requested Member or Administrator role, and the business reason for access. Never provide an invitation token. A deactivated membership is restored only through a fresh invitation and acceptance.

## Understand a relationship change

Only Phaeno can convert a Prospect organization to Customer or Partner, and only after the business relationship is approved. Conversion preserves the organization identifier, users, memberships, package grants, download history, and audit history.

Conversion does not automatically:

- create or authorize an order;
- grant additional package versions;
- configure prices, credit, offerings, or analyses;
- accept commercial terms on the organization’s behalf; or
- change a user’s role.

After conversion, Phaeno completes the applicable service and commercial setup before order actions become available. Until then, continue to use only the capabilities visible in the portal.

## Protect organization boundaries

Do not use another organization’s invitation, package link, downloaded file, or commercial reference. If the current organization name is wrong, stop before opening data and contact Phaeno.
