# Account and access

Your Portal access is created through an invitation to a specific Partner organization. The current organization and your role determine which reagent, assembly, data, and administrative actions are available.

## Accept an invitation

1. Open the newest invitation link sent for your Partner organization.
2. Sign in with the same email address that received the invitation.
3. Review the organization, your name, and the offered role.
4. Accept or decline the invitation.

Invitation links are private. A link can expire, be revoked, or be replaced by a newer invitation. If it is unavailable, ask your Partner administrator or Phaeno to send a new invitation.

The portal does not collect or reset your sign-in password. Use the account recovery offered by the identity service on the sign-in page.

## Confirm the current Partner organization

The user menu shows the organization active in your current session. That organization controls the offerings, negotiated prices, addresses, orders, assembly requests, data grants, and users you can see.

Self-service switching between multiple external organization memberships is not currently available. If the wrong organization is shown, stop before opening or creating work and contact Phaeno. Information and commercial terms never carry from one Partner organization to another.

## Understand roles

- **Member** can review the current Partner’s work and download files that have been released.
- **Administrator** can also place reagent orders, submit assembly requests, accept quotes or eligible commercial changes, request cancellations, approve substitutions, and manage membership.

## Manage Partner members

Partner administrators manage membership only for their current organization. In **User management**, active members and invited people appear in one list. A **Pending invitation** status means account registration is incomplete. Use the card’s actions menu to resend or revoke that invitation, edit an active member’s organization role, or deactivate a membership.

Enter the person’s first name, last name, and exact sign-in email, but never copy or request an invitation token. A deactivated membership is restored only through a fresh invitation and acceptance. Contact Phaeno for a globally disabled account or an action the organization administrator cannot complete.

## Resolve common access states

| What you see | What to do |
| --- | --- |
| Invitation link unavailable | Ask an administrator to confirm it is pending and resend it. Use only the newest link. |
| Email does not match | Sign in with the invited email or request a corrected invitation. |
| No active memberships | Ask your Partner administrator to coordinate a fresh invitation with Phaeno. |
| Wrong organization shown | Stop before opening records and contact Phaeno to verify the active membership. |
| Organization unavailable | Ask Phaeno whether the organization is inactive. |
| Account disabled | Contact Phaeno; a Partner administrator cannot override this state. |
| Offering or action missing | Confirm the current organization, then ask an administrator to review your role or Phaeno to review Partner eligibility. |

Never send a password, sign-in code, invitation token, or browser storage value in a support message.
