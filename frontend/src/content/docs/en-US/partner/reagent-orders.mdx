# Order reagents

Partner organization administrators order from offerings and negotiated prices configured specifically for their organization.

## Prepare a draft

1. Open **Reagent orders** and create a draft.
2. Add only products offered to the selected Partner organization.
3. Enter quantities that satisfy the offering’s minimum, maximum, and increment rules.
4. Select an active shipping address from the Partner address book.
5. Add any delivery note and the required purchase-order number.

You can save and resume a draft or create a new draft from a prior order. The new draft uses current availability and pricing rather than copying stale commercial terms.

## Place the order

Placement revalidates the item, Partner eligibility, quantity, active negotiated price, shipping address, and purchase-order number. The portal then freezes the offering, price, currency, address, and PO snapshot.

QuickBooks Online receives the actual negotiated price used for the order. Tax, freight, discounts, invoice totals, and billing-address facts remain authoritative in QuickBooks.

If QuickBooks is temporarily unavailable, the local order remains visible with a commercial-sync status. Phaeno can retry it without creating a duplicate order.

## Review substitutions and changes

Phaeno cannot fulfill a different reagent without explicit approval from a Partner organization administrator. A substitution request shows the original and proposed product and its commercial effect. Declining it preserves the original order facts.

Any post-placement revision that increases the total requires Partner-administrator approval before Phaeno accepts it.

## Track fulfillment

Orders can be partially shipped. Each shipment can show carrier, service, tracking information, quantities, lot, expiration, and packing-slip details. The order keeps shipped and remaining quantities visible, including backordered units.

## Cancel eligible work

A draft or an unaccepted placed order can be cancelled. After Phaeno accepts the order, submit a cancellation request. Shipped quantities are never erased, and approved commercial changes remain in QuickBooks Online.
