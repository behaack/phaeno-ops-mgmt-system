# Data Library, billing, and organization access

Partner access is divided among curated Phaeno data, operational Partner work, QuickBooks Online commercial documents, and organization membership.

## Use curated data

The Data Library contains only exact package versions that Phaeno explicitly granted to the selected Partner. Active members can review the package summary, quality information, manifest, and checksums, then download individual files or a complete archive.

Packages are read-only and remain separate from reagent and data-assembly records. A later package version does not replace an existing grant automatically.

Phaeno may revoke or quarantine access. Those actions block new portal viewing and downloads but cannot recall files already downloaded. Partner administrators receive safe follow-up guidance when action on prior downloads is required.

## Understand commercial documents

QuickBooks Online is authoritative for estimates, invoices, payment status, tax, freight, discounts, balances, and payment links. The portal shows synchronized status and links; it does not collect payment-card information.

Reagent pricing is negotiated per Partner and snapshotted when an order is placed. Data-assembly pricing is per job through an immutable quote. Assembly credit status is a separate audited setting that determines Net 30 or paid-before-download release.

## Manage the Partner team

The authorization model permits Partner organization administrators to invite people by email and assign member or administrator access only within their organization. Invitations are organization-specific and must be accepted by the invited email account.

The current **User management** screen is mock-backed and does not persist these actions. Contact Phaeno for durable changes until the connected administration screen is released. Membership deactivation preserves historical actions, and a fresh invitation is required for reactivation. Phaeno administrators control global account and organization status.

Partner administrators can review their organization’s curated-data download history. Operational downloads remain authorized against the selected Partner and the applicable release gate.
