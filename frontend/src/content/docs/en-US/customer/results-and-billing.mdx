# Results and billing

Scientific readiness and commercial release are separate. The portal always shows whether a result is still being produced, ready but commercially held, or available to download.

## Follow sample progress

Open a laboratory job to see each sample’s current state. Receipt, accessioning, laboratory work, data processing, result readiness, and completion are recorded independently so one delayed sample does not hide progress on the others.

## Understand the release rule

- **Approved Customer credit:** Net 30 terms apply. Phaeno may release a ready sample’s files after the required commercial synchronization, even while other samples remain in progress.
- **No approved Customer credit:** the portal may show that results are ready, but no job result can be downloaded until the completed job’s linked QuickBooks Online invoice is confirmed paid.

Credit status is maintained by authorized Phaeno users and is not changed from the Customer workspace.

## Download released results

1. Open the laboratory job and select the sample.
2. Confirm that the result is marked available rather than payment-required or synchronization-pending.
3. Review the release details and download the available files.

Each release is immutable and linked to its scientific and quality facts. A corrected result is delivered as a new release; it does not replace the audit history of an earlier release.

Downloads require an active membership in the organization that owns the job and are audited.

## Use QuickBooks documents

QuickBooks Online is the authority for estimates, invoices, Net 30 terms, tax, freight, discounts, balances, and paid status. The portal displays synchronized status and links to QuickBooks-hosted documents or payment pages. Payment-card information is not collected by Phaeno Portal.

If a commercial synchronization is pending or failed, the local job remains recorded. Phaeno can retry the integration without creating a duplicate job or document.
