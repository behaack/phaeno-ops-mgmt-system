# Data Library and organization access

Your Customer organization can use Phaeno-curated data packages that Phaeno has explicitly assigned to it. These packages are separate from laboratory job inputs and results.

## Use the Data Library

The Data Library shows only active grants for the selected organization. Each grant points to an exact, immutable package version. A later package publication does not silently change the version your organization received.

You can review the package summary, scientific context, quality information, file manifest, and checksums. Active organization members can download individual files or a complete archive. The archive includes the manifest and every file in that version.

Curated packages are read-only. Customer users cannot edit, republish, or grant them to another organization.

## Understand access changes

Phaeno may grant, upgrade, revoke, quarantine, or retire a package. Revocation or quarantine blocks new viewing and downloads but cannot recall copies already downloaded. If a prior download needs action, the portal gives organization administrators Customer-safe guidance.

Organization administrators can review download history for their own organization. They cannot see another organization’s activity.

## Manage organization members

The authorization model permits Customer organization administrators to invite members by email, choose member or administrator access, change eligible roles, and deactivate memberships only within their organization. Invitations must be accepted by the invited email account.

The current **User management** screen is mock-backed and does not persist these actions. Contact Phaeno for durable changes. Deactivation ends portal access without deleting the person or audit history, and restoring an inactive membership requires a fresh invitation. Phaeno administrators handle global account disablement and organization status.

Every user should have their own account. Do not share sign-in credentials or use a shared mailbox as a substitute for an accountable user.
