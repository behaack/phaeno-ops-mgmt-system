# Getting started

Portal keeps your organization’s laboratory requests, sample progress, released results, commercial documents, curated data, and user access in one place.

## Navigate the help center

Guide navigation opens beneath the toolbar from the far-left edge of the screen. Its header identifies Documentation and, on wider screens, provides the pin or unpin icon. Pointer users can preview an unpinned sidebar by approaching the left edge; keyboard and touch users open it from the tab. Choosing a guide closes an unpinned sidebar and displays the article.

## Confirm the current organization

The user menu shows the organization active in your current session. That organization controls which records and actions you can see. Customer information never carries across organizations.

Self-service switching between multiple external organization memberships is not currently available. If the wrong organization is active, or your account belongs to more than one external organization, contact Phaeno before continuing.

## Know your access level

Customer organization administrators can create and submit laboratory service requests, accept quotes, request cancellations, and manage organization members. Other active members can review their organization’s jobs, follow progress, and download results that have been released.

If an expected action is missing, first confirm the organization shown in the user menu. Your organization administrator can then check your membership.

## Find your work

- **Dashboard** provides a starting point for the current organization.
- **Lab services** contains laboratory requests, quotes, sample progress, and results.
- **Data Library** contains Phaeno-curated data packages explicitly assigned to your organization.
- For organization administrators, **User management** manages durable invitations, Member or Organization administrator roles, and membership deactivation.
- **Docs** opens these guides.

## Keep submissions safe

The initial laboratory request workflow is not a protected-health-information intake workflow. Do not enter patient names, dates of birth, medical record numbers, or other direct identifiers in request fields, notes, file names, or uploads.

Use your organization’s own reference values and follow the instructions attached to the selected analysis.

## Get help

When contacting Phaeno, provide the portal request or sample reference and a description of the step you were performing. Do not send credentials, authentication tokens, or sensitive sample content in a support message.
