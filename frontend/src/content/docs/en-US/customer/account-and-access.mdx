# Account and access

Your Phaeno Portal access is created through an organization invitation. The selected organization and your role determine which Customer records and actions are available.

## Accept an invitation

1. Open the newest invitation link sent for your organization.
2. Sign in with the same email address that received the invitation.
3. Review the organization, your name, and the offered role.
4. Accept or decline the invitation.

An invitation link is private and can expire, be revoked, or be replaced by a newer link. Do not forward it. If a link is unavailable, ask your organization administrator or Phaeno to send a new invitation.

The portal does not collect or reset your sign-in password. Complete sign-in and account recovery through the identity service shown on the sign-in page.

## Choose the correct organization

If you belong to more than one organization, use the organization switcher in the user menu before opening a job or Data Library record. The selected organization scopes every portal read and action.

If an expected record or action is missing, confirm the selected organization before reporting a problem.

## Understand roles

- **Member** can review the selected Customer organization’s work and download files that have been released.
- **Administrator** can also create and submit laboratory requests, accept quotes, request cancellations, and manage organization membership.

Actions remain hidden or unavailable when the selected organization or your role does not permit them. Phaeno can assist with access, but it cannot bypass the organization boundary to expose another Customer’s records.

## Manage Customer members

The authorization model allows Customer administrators to manage membership only for their selected organization. The current **User management** screen is a session-only preview: changes made there are not sent to the durable invitation and membership APIs.

Until the connected administration screen is released, contact Phaeno for durable invitation or membership changes. Provide the organization and person’s exact email, but never an invitation token. The durable workflow supports resending or revoking pending invitations, membership deactivation, and reactivation only through a fresh invitation and acceptance.

## Resolve common access states

| What you see | What to do |
| --- | --- |
| Invitation link unavailable | Ask an administrator to confirm the invitation is still pending and resend it. Use only the newest link. |
| Email does not match | Sign out and use the email address that received the invitation, or request a corrected invitation. |
| No active memberships | Ask your Customer administrator to coordinate a fresh invitation with Phaeno. |
| Organization unavailable | Confirm the selected organization and ask Phaeno whether the organization is inactive. |
| Account disabled | Contact Phaeno. A Customer administrator cannot override a globally disabled account. |
| Expected action missing | Confirm the selected organization, then ask an administrator to review your role. |

Never send a password, sign-in code, invitation token, or browser storage value in a support message.
