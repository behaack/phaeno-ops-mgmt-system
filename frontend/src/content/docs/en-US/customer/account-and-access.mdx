# Account and access

Your Portal access is created through an organization invitation. The current organization and your role determine which Customer records and actions are available.

## Accept an invitation

1. Open the newest invitation link sent for your organization.
2. Sign in with the same email address that received the invitation.
3. Review the organization, your name, and the offered role.
4. Accept or decline the invitation.

An invitation link is private and can expire, be revoked, or be replaced by a newer link. Do not forward it. If a link is unavailable, ask your organization administrator or Phaeno to send a new invitation.

The portal does not collect or reset your sign-in password. Complete sign-in and account recovery through the identity service shown on the sign-in page.

## Confirm the current organization

The user menu shows the organization active in your current session. That organization scopes every portal read and action.

Self-service switching between multiple external organization memberships is not currently available. If the wrong organization is shown, stop before opening a job or Data Library record and contact Phaeno. If an expected record or action is missing, confirm the current organization before reporting a problem.

## Understand roles

- **Member** can review the current Customer organization’s work and download files that have been released.
- **Administrator** can also create and submit laboratory requests, accept quotes, request cancellations, and manage organization membership.

Actions remain hidden or unavailable when the current organization or your role does not permit them. Phaeno can assist with access, but it cannot bypass the organization boundary to expose another Customer’s records.

## Manage Customer members

Customer administrators manage membership only for their current organization. In **User management**, active members and invited people appear in one list. A **Pending invitation** status means account registration is incomplete. Use the card’s actions menu to resend or revoke that invitation, edit an active member’s organization role, or deactivate a membership.

Enter the person’s first name, last name, and exact sign-in email, but never copy or request an invitation token. A deactivated membership is restored only through a fresh invitation and acceptance. Contact Phaeno for a globally disabled account or an action the organization administrator cannot complete.

## Resolve common access states

| What you see | What to do |
| --- | --- |
| Invitation link unavailable | Ask an administrator to confirm the invitation is still pending and resend it. Use only the newest link. |
| Email does not match | Sign out and use the email address that received the invitation, or request a corrected invitation. |
| No active memberships | Ask your Customer administrator to coordinate a fresh invitation with Phaeno. |
| Wrong organization shown | Stop before opening records and contact Phaeno to verify the active membership. |
| Organization unavailable | Ask Phaeno whether the organization is inactive. |
| Account disabled | Contact Phaeno. A Customer administrator cannot override a globally disabled account. |
| Expected action missing | Confirm the current organization, then ask an administrator to review your role. |

Never send a password, sign-in code, invitation token, or browser storage value in a support message.
